Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3 2025

1. Who we are

FindMyClass (“we,” “our,” “Service”) is a student‑built web and mobile application that helps California State University, Northridge (CSUN) users navigate to classrooms and manage class schedules.

2. Information we collect

CategoryExamplesPurpose / Legal basis*
Account dataUsername, hashed passwordContract (provide Service)
Schedule dataClass names, times, building & room IDsContract
Location dataGPS coordinates while navigation is activeConsent; Legitimate interest
Device & usageBrowser type, OS, IP, error logsLegitimate interest (analytics, security)
Cookies / local storageAuth token, preferencesContract (stay logged‑in)

* GDPR Article 6 legal bases.

3. How we use your information

  • Provide indoor/outdoor turn‑by‑turn directions
  • Sync & display your saved schedule
  • Improve accuracy & performance via aggregated analytics
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Sharing & disclosures

We do not sell personal information. We share only with:

  • Service providers (AWS, Google Maps) bound by confidentiality
  • Campus data providers that supply building/classroom coordinates
  • Law enforcement or regulators when legally compelled

5. Retention

Account and schedule data are kept until you delete your account or 24 months after last activity. Logs may be stored up to 6 years.

6. Your rights

  • Access / export your data
  • Correct inaccurate info
  • Delete (“right to be forgotten”)
  • Opt‑out of sale/sharing (we don’t sell, but you may request restriction)
  • Non‑discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

Email requests to privacy@FindMyClass.app. We respond within 45 days.

7. Security

We use TLS 1.3 encryption, salted bcrypt password hashing, IAM‑scoped keys, and periodic penetration tests. No method is 100 % secure.

8. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13. If we learn we collected data from a child under 13 without parental consent, we delete it.

9. International users

Servers are in the United States. By using the Service you consent to processing and storage in the U.S. and any other country where we or our providers operate.

10. Changes to this policy

Material changes will be emailed to registered users or highlighted in‑app 30 days before taking effect.
